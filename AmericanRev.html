The American Revolution



There is quite a lot of relationship actually. During the Protestant Reformation, many Puritans started
to move to the Americas in search of religious freedom. They didn't like how the churches in England
were too Catholic. An example is the Mayflower, where Puritans were treated badly by King James I,
so they sailed to America. This mindset of wanting freedom and independence later on spread to the
American Revolution! The 13 colonies started to form because of this.
